Geography Optional Syllabus

The UPSC Geography Optional syllabus is divided into Paper I (Principles of Geography) and Paper II (Geography of India). Detailed topics include:

  • Physical Geography - Geomorphology, Climatology, Oceanography, Biogeography
  • Human Geography - Cultural, Economic, Settlement Geography
  • Geography of India - Resources, Agriculture, Industries, Population, Regional Development

Preparation Strategy

Start with NCERTs, move to standard books like Savindra Singh, Majid Husain, and GC Leong. Practice maps, answer writing, and previous year questions.
